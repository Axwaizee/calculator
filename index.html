Functions :
  • This calculator can be accessed by both click and keyboard.

  • Keyboard Keys Functions :
    • 0-9 and . for entering numbers and decimal.
    • +, - ,* ,/ , = and Enter for doing functions.
    • C and c for clearing everything.
    • Backspace for clearing last operator or last digit.

  • Mouse Click Functions :
    • You can click on the objects to do their respective functions.
    • You can also access some extra functions by clicking as Keyboard doesn't support those symbols.
    • You can also click on the result/problem window to copy the whole text.

  • Mobile View :
    • This includes all the functions of Mouse Click Functions.
